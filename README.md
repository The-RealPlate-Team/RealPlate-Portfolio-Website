# RealPlate Portfolio Website

A professional portfolio website dedicated entirely to **RealPlate**, a restaurant‑simulation game I am developing. This site is designed for **college applications, internships, and early job opportunities**, providing a clear and organized presentation of my technical skills, design process, and long‑term project development.

---

## 📌 Purpose of This Website

This portfolio serves as a focused showcase of my work on RealPlate. Instead of a general multi‑project portfolio, this site highlights a **single, in‑depth project** to demonstrate:

- My ability to plan and execute a long‑term software project  
- My understanding of UI/UX and front‑end development  
- My game design and system‑building skills  
- My ability to document and communicate technical work  
- My consistency and growth as a developer  

The goal is to give admissions teams and hiring managers a clear look at how I think, build, and iterate.

---

## 🎮 About RealPlate

**RealPlate** is a restaurant‑simulation game centered around creativity, management, and player‑driven progression. The project includes:

- Core gameplay systems (cooking, serving, management)  
- UI/UX design for menus and interactions  
- Visual design and asset planning  
- Feature roadmapping and iteration  
- Long‑term development planning  

RealPlate is an ongoing project, and this website acts as its public development hub.
